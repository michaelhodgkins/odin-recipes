Burrito

Here is a photo of a delicious burrito recipe

This is a family favourite recipe for all ages and will be loved by anyone, if you are having a dinner party or two

This recipe does not require too nuch time or ingrediants but love and affection.
If you have never tried rolling your own burrito before this would be the perfect time for you to dip your toes into the water and give it a shot

Ingrediants

  • Tortilla
  • Black Beans
  • Vegan Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Cooked brown rice

Steps to make the Burrito

  1. Drain and rinse the black beans and the corn. In a medium bowl, combine them with the paprika powder, cumin, smoked paprika powder, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
    Add the chopped green onions and the fresh cilantro and stir well.
  2. Lay a tortilla down flat on a plate. Add some rice in the center of the tortilla. Then add some of the bean corn salad on top.
    Next comes some salsa, lettuce, vegan cheese, vegan sour cream, and avocado. If you want you could also sprinkle some red pepper flakes on top.
  3. Fold in the sides and then roll the burrito up and wrap it in foil. Then cut it into half with a sharp knife. Enjoy!
